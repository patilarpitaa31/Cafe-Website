Our Philosophy

Some places serve coffee. We serve time — stretched, softened, savored.
Overtime Café began with a simple thought: what if there was a place made for the moments that don’t fit neatly into the schedule? The in-betweens. The afters. The pauses we crave when the world is still moving.
Overtime is a space for pauses that don’t follow the clock. For comfort that feels familiar, and company that doesn’t need words.
Here, time softens.
Flavors linger.
And every visit becomes a memory — slow, warm, and just a little past closing time.

Simply Overtime

Because some flavors linger… even after closing time.
At Overtime Café, we believe in slow moments and meaningful tastes.
Our specialties are crafted with intention — familiar, comforting, and quietly memorable.
From our Signature Coffee, brewed for thoughtful pauses,
to the warmth of a Cinnamon Roll shared in silence,
every dish tells a quiet story.
Some are sweet, like our Red Velvet Chocolate Cupcake.
Some are nostalgic, like a flaky Chicken Pie or a soft Walnut Fudge.
All are made to stay with you — even when the evening ends.
A little warmth. A little memory. A little Overtime.
